About Me

I love communications. I love technology. Human language and communications are constantly changing and I love the way technology and the Internet have cranked this change up to warp speed. I grew up in a newspaper family; I still read several newspapers per day only now I read them on my smartphone.


In college I studied English (because I love to read) and Journalism (see above). I spent five years in the Army bouncing around the world. When I returned to civilian life I got an MBA and eventually landed in digital marketing, ecommerce, digital engagement, or whatever the buzzword of the moment is (remember what I said above about language constantly changing?). This led me to dig deeper into the technology that drives global communication and commerce. So here we are. More languages. More technology. Coding.
